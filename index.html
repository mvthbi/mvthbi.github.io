Who I am

A data person with a
storyteller's soul.

I'm a Senior Analytics Engineer originally from Indonesia, now based in Berlin. My work lives at the intersection of rigorous data craft and practical business thinking — I care about building analytics foundations that teams actually trust and use.

Most recently at Contentful, I worked on data models, metrics pipelines, and self-serve analytics for a global B2B SaaS product. I'm drawn to data-mature, product-led companies where analytics genuinely shapes decisions.

Outside of work, I'm a mother of two, a home cook (Indonesian food is always on the menu), and someone who thinks carefully about what it means to build a meaningful career and life abroad. I write about all of it.
